Data

House
House Level

Organized collection of House level data represented in the form of infographics, charts and tables for better understanding of municipal infrastructure and service. Representation of this data is an attempt to enhance the spatial and socio-economic relationship between the structure where ward inhabitants reside and work and the services provided by the municipality.

Household
Household Level

Organized collection of household level data represented in the form of infographics, charts and tables for better understanding of municipal infrastructure and services towards the families. Representation of this data is an attempt to enhance the spatial and socio-economic relationship between each household and the services provided by the municipality.

Individual
Individual Level

Organized collection of Individual level data represented in the form of infographics, charts and tables for better understanding of municipal infrastructure and services towards them. Representation of this data is an attempt to enhance the spatial and socio-economic relationship between the inhabitants of each structure/member of household where they either reside or work, and the services provided by the municipality.

Absentee
Absentee Level

Organized collection of absentee data represented in the form of infographics charts and tables for better understanding of municipal infrastructure and services. Representation of this data is an attempt to enhance the spatial and socio-economic relationship between the municipal services and population that are a member of the household surveyed and legally present but absent at the time of data collection.

Business
Business Level

Organized collection of business data represented in the form of infographics, charts and tables for better understanding of municipal infrastructure and services towards their growth. Representation of this data is an attempt to enhance the spatial and socio-economic relationship between the municipal services and businesses that are a critical part of the municipality’s local economic development.

Damak Municipality

Damak is one of the oldest municipalities in Jhapa District in Province 1 of Nepal. It is situated between the Ratuwa River in the east and the Maawa River in the west. It has Sivalik Hills in its north and ends with the intersection of Ratuwa River and Mawa River in the south. Mahendra Highway (longest highway of Nepal) crosses this municipality nearly bisecting it. It is the largest city in Jhapa District as well as in Mechi zone.
